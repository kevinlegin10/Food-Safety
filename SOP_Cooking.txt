STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: POULTRY COOKING
Batch cooking vats must reach a minimum internal temperature of 165°F.
Once 165°F is reached, the temperature must be maintained for exactly 3 minutes.
If the temperature falls below 165°F during the 3-minute hold, or fails to reach 165°F, the batch fails the critical limit. Failed batches must be immediately quarantined and flagged for reprocessing. No exceptions.
